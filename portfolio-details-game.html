DisNats School Escape: A Game-based Learning Tool with an Escape Simulator for Disasters Emergency-response and Risk Management for Don Honorio Ventura State University

An educational game and escape simulator created for Don Honorio Ventura State University, This application involves three campuses: Candaba Campus, Mexico Campus, and Sto. Tomas Campus, due to resource constraints and time limitations in designing and developing the game. The game's central theme is to incorporate a range of disasters, including fires, earthquakes, and storms, which players must navigate and escape from. These disasters should be portrayed in a realistic and accurate manner, complete with suitable sound and visual effects. Additionally, the game aims to offer realistic environments that faithfully represent different college campus locations, such as classrooms, libraries, and computer laboratories.
